# 2026 Free Agent Class Analysis

The 2026 UFA/RFA class features several high-profile names. Here's what our model projects, and where the real value might be hiding.

## Top UFAs

| Player | Age | Current AAV | Projected AAV | WAR |
|--------|-----|-------------|---------------|-----|
| Nikita Kucherov | 33 | $9.5M | $11.2M | 6.14 |
| Sidney Crosby | 39 | $8.7M | $7.7M | 2.69 |
| Alex Ovechkin | 40 | $9.5M | $7.0M | 2.66 |
| Mark Stone | 34 | $9.5M | $7.0M | 3.59 |
| Nico Hischier | 27 | $7.25M | $10.1M | 1.88 |

**Kucherov** is the crown jewel. At 33, he's still elite (6.14 WAR this season) but age will suppress his term. Expect a 4-5 year deal in the $11-12M range.

**Crosby and Ovechkin** are legacy signings. Both will likely take hometown discounts on short-term deals. Our model projects $7-8M, but these are less about market value and more about the franchise relationship.

**Hischier** is the best value UFA under 30. At 27, he's entering his peak and should command $10M+.

## Top RFAs

| Player | Age | Current AAV | Projected AAV | WAR |
|--------|-----|-------------|---------------|-----|
| Jason Robertson | 27 | $7.75M | $12.4M | 4.95 |
| Connor Bedard | 21 | $950K | $8.3M | 0.74 |
| Logan Cooley | 22 | $950K | $7.6M | 2.02 |
| Adam Fantilli | 21 | $950K | $8.4M | 1.45 |

**Robertson** is the most interesting case. His current $7.75M deal was a bridge — the model projects $12.4M on a 7-year extension. With 4.95 WAR this season, he has the production to justify it.

**Bedard, Cooley, and Fantilli** are all coming off ELCs. Their first big contracts will be fascinating tests of how teams value young talent vs proven production. The model projects $7-8M range, but RFA leverage could push these lower.

## Where's the Value?

The biggest projected raises (current → projected):
- **Jackson LaCombe**: $925K → $8.7M (+$7.8M) — breakout defensive season
- **Dylan Holloway**: $2.3M → $8.1M (+$5.8M) — emerging top-6 forward
- **Pavel Dorofeyev**: $1.8M → $7.8M (+$5.9M) — elite production on a cheap deal

## Contract Strategy

For GMs looking to sign UFAs:
- **Target Hischier** — prime age, moderate current AAV, should be a relative bargain vs younger RFA comparables
- **Avoid long-term deals for 33+ UFAs** — Kucherov, Stone, and others will decline on the back end of any 5+ year deal
- **Pay your RFAs early** — Robertson, Bedard, and Fantilli are only getting more expensive. Bridge deals just delay the inevitable
